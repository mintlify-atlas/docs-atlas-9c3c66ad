## Get your icons in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your icons">
    Pick the technology IDs you want to display from the [full icons list](/icons/list). Each icon has a short ID like `js`, `react`, `docker`, or `kubernetes`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your URL">
    Combine the base URL with your comma-separated icon IDs:
    ```
    https://skillicons.dev/icons?i=js,ts,react,nodejs,docker
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Embed it">
    Paste the URL as a Markdown image or an HTML `<img>` tag — it works anywhere SVG images are rendered.
    ```md
    ![My Skills](https://skillicons.dev/icons?i=js,ts,react,nodejs,docker)
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
